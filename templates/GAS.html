Gas

A gas is a state of matter with no fixed shape or volume. The density of gases is lower than that of other states of matter, such as solids and liquids. There is a lot of empty space between particles that have a lot of kinetic energy but aren't particularly attracted to each other.

Example: oxygen, ozone
